Reducing Dust Emissions in Cement Manufacturing

← Back to Home

📌 Project Summary

Used data science techniques to reduce dust emissions from a cement packaging plant by identifying key influencing parameters and optimizing operational settings.

🔍 Problem

The cement packaging plant was emitting high levels of dust into the atmosphere, leading to environmental and health concerns.

🛠 Approach

  • Analyzed historical plant data using SQL and Python
  • Identified differential pressure as the most impactful parameter
  • Built a linear regression model to determine optimal settings
  • Developed a simple recommendation tool to guide operational decisions

📊 Results

Dust emissions were reduced by over 16%, improving compliance with environmental regulations and enhancing workplace safety.

🧰 Tools Used

  • SQL (data cleaning and grouping)
  • Python (Pandas, Seaborn, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib)
  • Jupyter Notebook (Analysis & Modeling)
